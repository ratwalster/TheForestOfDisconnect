Privacy Policy for The Forest of Disconnect

Last Updated: June 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to *The Forest of Disconnect*, an interactive storytelling project hosted by InnerVerse (the "Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you opt in to receive updates related to the project.

2. Information We Collect

When you opt in via our Opt-In Page, we collect:

  • Your phone number, provided voluntarily to receive SMS updates or audio notifications.
  • Consent status, indicating your agreement to receive messages.

We do not collect additional personal data unless required for future features.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used solely to:

  • Send updates, reflections, or chapter releases for *The Forest of Disconnect*.
  • Notify you of new story content or interactive prompts (if SMS is enabled in the future).

Currently, Twilio is used to generate audio content, and no SMS messages are sent unless explicitly expanded.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your phone number and consent status are stored securely on our servers. We do not share your information with third parties. Data is retained as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose of opt-in or as required by law, and you can request deletion by contacting us.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message (if SMS is implemented).
  • Request access to or deletion of your data by emailing innerverseportmanteau@gmail.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our project evolves. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

For questions or concerns, please contact us at innerverseportmanteau@gmail.com.

8. Disclaimer

This Privacy Policy is provided for transparency and is not legal advice. For specific legal concerns, consult a professional.
